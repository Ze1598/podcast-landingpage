The Experiment in Progress

I'm documenting my journey testing frameworks from psychology, philosophy, business, and personal development - showing what works, what's bs, and what I'm still figuring out.

This started as an experiment itself: turning my essays into podcast conversations using Google's NotebookLM. A year in, it's time to bring more humans into the loop.

Episodes include:

  • Experiments I'm running on myself
  • Results from what I've tested
  • Fresh perspectives and ideas that spark new experiments
  • Conversations with people further ahead who have results to share

This is a documentary of someone stumbling through progress and bringing on guests who have their own experiments to share.

If you're tired of collecting motivational quotes and want to see what happens when someone finally moves into action - that's what this is.

What you can expect as a guest

Before booking

  • Typically, guests will email directly with an inquiry to be a guest before booking, so feel free to reach out via email or LinkedIn for anything you might need ahead of booking the episode

After booking

  • Hello email from me to align on the topics for discussion (especially important if we have not spoken prior)

On the day

  • Before we hit recording: Initial greetings to get into the flow of the conversation
  • During recording: This will be the episode itself, which can be as long as desired within that booking slot
  • After recording: We stop recording and have a short debrief before leaving the call

After the recording

  • I will send a link to a Google Drive folder that includes the episode recording, transcript, video shorts with captions, as well as the tentative episode title and description for your validation - within 48 hours of the recording
  • Once we are happy with everything, the episode is released on the agreed date
  • Promotional materials include posts on social media (LinkedIn, Substack) and video shorts (TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn)
